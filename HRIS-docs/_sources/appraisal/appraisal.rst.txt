Appraisal 
************************

The appraisal module makes it easy to automate appraisal process. The HR needs to setup a questionnaire and define the hierarchy of who will answer which questions among the appraisee, recommender, approver and HR personnel, then the system can handle the process on its own.

We offer customized reports to see a summary of the appraisal process for HR.
