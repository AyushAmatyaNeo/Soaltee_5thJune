Travel balance and Synergy integration
***************************************

Those companies that are using our HRIS with ITN Synergy, can have functionality to view ledger impact on  synergy table.All the amount of travel advance and travel expenses requested are stored and calculated appropriately in our synergy table.

There are three conditions in which debit and credit are balanced in synergy table.

1.Expense higher than advance requested(Excess condition)

for eg:advance requested:16,000

       expense requested:20,000

.. figure:: img/synergy_excess.png
   :scale: 50%

   Excess condition Ledger impact on synergy table.


2.Expense equal to advance requested(Exact condition)

for eg:advance requested:9,000

       expense requested:9,000

.. figure:: img/synergy_exact.png
   :scale: 50%

   Exact condition Ledger impact on synergy table.


3.Expense less than advance requested(less condition)

for eg:advance requested:20,000

       expense requested:13,000

.. figure:: img/synergy_less.png
   :scale: 50%

   Less condition Ledger impact on synergy table.
